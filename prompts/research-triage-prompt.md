# Research Triage Prompt

## Purpose

Turn a scientific paper abstract into a structured research summary for an initial review.

## Instructions

Analyse only the information contained in the title and abstract provided.

Do not invent missing methods, results or conclusions. Clearly state when information is not available.

Present the response using the following structure:

### Plain-English summary

Explain the research in clear language without removing important scientific meaning.

### Biological problem

Describe the biological process, disease, target or question being investigated.

### Methods

List the main experimental or computational methods mentioned in the abstract.

### Main findings

Summarise the results reported by the researchers.

### Possible relevance to drug discovery

Explain how the research could relate to areas such as target identification, target validation, biomarkers, treatment development or understanding disease mechanisms.

Do not claim that the study has direct drug-discovery value unless the abstract supports this.

### Limitations and unanswered questions

Identify limitations mentioned in the abstract and questions that cannot be answered from the information provided.

### Verification checklist

List the main claims that a human reviewer should check against the original paper.
